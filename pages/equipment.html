Equipment Recommendations

Required Equipment

Item Specification Notes
Air Rifle PCP, mag-fed (10+ shots) Modern <12 ft/lbs PCP capable of 90m shots
Caliber .22 cal or .177 cal Both suitable for TAC12
Optic Telescopic FFP preferred but not essential

Recommended Features

  • FFP (First Focal Plane) Optics: Preferred for versatility across magnification ranges
  • Magazine Capacity: Minimum 10 shots, larger mags advantageous
  • Accuracy: Capable of sub-1.5mil groups
  • Range: Must be capable of 90m shots

Competition vs Training

Important

Springers are welcome to train, but PRS competition stages must be shot with a magazine-fed PCP Airgun.
