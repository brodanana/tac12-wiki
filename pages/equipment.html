Equipment

What you need to compete at TAC12

Required Equipment

Item Specification Notes
Air Rifle PCP, magazine fed (10+ shots) Modern <12 ft/lbs PCP capable of 90m
Caliber .22 cal or .177 cal Both suitable
Optic Telescopic FFP preferred, not essential

Recommended Features

🎯

FFP Optics

First Focal Plane scopes maintain correct reticle scale at all magnification levels. Preferred but not required.

📊

Accuracy

Must be capable of sub-1.5mil groups at distance. Consistent accuracy wins stages.

🎖️

Magazine Capacity

Minimum 10 shots. Larger magazines mean fewer reloads during timed stages.

Competition vs Training

⚠️ Equipment Rules

Springers are welcome for training, but PRS competition stages must be shot with a magazine-fed PCP Airgun.
