What is TAC12?

Precision Rifle Series adapted for the UK power limit. Shoot 20m to 90m in timed stages against props and gongs.

🎯

PRS Style Competition

Multi-prop stages with gongs at varying distances. Time limits add challenge to your precision shooting.

Entry Requirements

Everything you need to compete at TAC12

🎖️ Equipment

  • PCP Airgun, magazine fed (10+ shots)
  • Telescopic optic (FFP preferred)
  • Modern <12 ft/lbs PCP capable of 90m
  • .22 cal or .177 cal

📊 Ability

  • 1.5 mil group size ability
  • Comfortable with timed stages
  • Multiple shooting positions
  • Ready to compete

📋 Rules

  • PRS stages require mag-fed PCP
  • Springers welcome for training
  • Under 18s must have guardian
  • Follow all safety protocols
