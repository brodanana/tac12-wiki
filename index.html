Welcome to TAC12

Tactical Airgun Challenge (TAC12) is a PRS (Precision Rifle Series) style precision shooting format adapted for the UK power limit of under 12 ft/lbs PCP Air Rifles. Running since November 2024 at Woodend Farm Complex.

🎯 Competition Format

PRS style stages with multiple props, gongs at all distances, and time limits. Distances from 20m to 90m.
