Competition Format & Rules

PRS Style Competition

TAC12 follows a PRS (Precision Rifle Series) style format, featuring:

  • Multiple Props: Various shooting positions and obstacles
  • Gongs at All Distances: Targets placed at varying distances
  • Time Limits: Stages are timed to add challenge

Shooting Distances

Targets range from 20m to 90m, testing your precision at various ranges.

Shooting Positions

Competitors will encounter multiple positions including:

  • Bench rest
  • Prone
  • Standing
  • Various positional challenges

Equipment Requirements

Important Note on Springers

Springers are welcome to train, but PRS stages must be shot with a magazine-fed PCP Airgun.

Entry Requirements

Requirement Details
Airgun Type PCP, magazine fed (at least 10 shots)
Optic Telescopic (ideally FFP, but not essential)
Ability 1.5 mil group size ability
