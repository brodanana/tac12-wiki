Safety Protocols

⚠️ Safety is Top Priority

Staff will help shooters become familiar with equipment. Please follow all safety protocols at all times.

Age Requirements

All UNDER 18s must be accompanied by a parent or guardian at all times.

Core Safety Rules

  1. Always assume the gun is loaded
  2. Magazine must be removed when moving from shooting bench
  3. Muzzle always pointed at floor when moving
  4. Never remove guns from cases outside of designated ranges

Range Rules

Rule Details
People per Lane Max 2 people per lane
Guns per Lane 1 gun per lane at any time
Prohibited Items No alcohol or drugs
Behavior Disruptive behavior not tolerated

Key Safety Principles

  • Treat every firearm as if it were loaded
  • Keep your finger off the trigger until ready to shoot
  • Always be aware of your muzzle direction
  • Follow all range officer instructions immediately
