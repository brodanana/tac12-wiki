🎯 Target Sports Facilities

TAC12 operates within Woodend Farm Complex's 5-range facility:

🔫

Airgun Ranges

3 ranges for rifle and pistol. Indoor 20m with 6 lanes, outdoor 50m with 8 lanes and knockdown targets.

🏹

Archery & Crossbow

100m outdoor archery and crossbow range with covered firing line.

🪓

Knife & Axe Throwing

Outdoor knife and axe throwing range for a unique target sport experience.
