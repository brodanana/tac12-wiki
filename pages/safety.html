Safety

Top priority at all times

⚠️ Safety is Top Priority

Staff will help shooters become familiar with equipment. Please follow all safety protocols at all times.

👤 Age Requirements

All UNDER 18s must be accompanied by a parent or guardian at all times on the range.

🔫 Core Safety Rules

1

Assume Loaded

Always treat your airgun as if it were loaded. Never point at anything you don't intend to shoot.

2

Remove Magazine

Magazine must be removed when moving from shooting bench. Keep muzzle pointed down.

3

Muzzle Down

Muzzle always pointed at floor when moving. Never sweep the range.

4

Case Only

Never remove guns from cases outside of designated ranges. Unsure? Ask a staff member.

📋 Range Rules

Rule Details
People per Lane Max 2 people per lane
Guns per Lane 1 gun per lane at any time
Alcohol/Drugs Strictly prohibited
Behavior Disruptive behavior will not be tolerated

🎯 Key Safety Principles

  • Treat every airgun as if it were loaded
  • Keep your finger off the trigger until ready to shoot
  • Always be aware of your muzzle direction
  • Follow all range officer instructions immediately
  • When in doubt, ask - staff are there to help
